User Stories
------------------------------------

As a client,  so I can keep my money safe , I would like to deposit an amount in to an account.
As a client,  so I can access my money when I need to,  I would like to be able to withdraw an amount.
As a client,  so I can keep track of my money over time,  I would like to see a dated history of deposits and withdrawals.

Objects      | Messages
------------------------------------
Account	     | have a balance
	           | have an activity history
	           | print statement (formatted history)
Deposit      | have an amount
	           | have a date
Withdrawal	 | have an amount
	           | have a date
